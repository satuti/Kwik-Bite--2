The Business

Kwik Bite is a fast food delivery service dedicated to bringing some of your favorite foods to your doorstep. Our goal is save you from having to leave your house to get food that isn't being delivered. So if you're at a party... party on cause we'll deliver what you need for a fee of $4.67. We currently only deliver to the champlin/ brooklyn park area. We also deliver to workers at select stores of arbor lakes in maple grove.
